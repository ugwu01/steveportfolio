About Me

I'm a data analyst, with over 5 years of experience in the FMCG and food processing industry. I possess extensive experience in data and process analytics with proficiency in Python, Power BI, Tableau, Spreadsheet and all dialects of SQL. When I’m not generating insights from big data, I am implementing solutions approved by executive management after reviewing the insight from my analysis.

I had my first degree in industrial chemistry and education, then proceeded to working with a leading food processing and manufacturing company in Africa before my drive for behind-the-scenes answers led me to data, working with data has incredibly broadened my horizon and satisfied my ever-curious mind.

Data analytics to me is like working behind-the-scenes of a block buster movie production or a backroom staff of a football team. I love working behind the scenes of big business decisions knowing I’m a huge influence in the decision-making process which is not based on opinions but entirely on metrics and insights.

Outside of analyzing data, I love seeing movies and watching live football matches. I enjoy sharing and exchanging knowledge with willing persons.
